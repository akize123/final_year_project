Why AUCA Connect

Everything you need to
manage academic research

One unified platform for submitting, reviewing, publishing, and discovering scholarly work across AUCA's academic community.

Structured Publishing
Submit research papers, theses, dissertations, and institutional project reports with guided metadata and formatting.
Peer Discovery
Browse, search, and filter publications across all AUCA departments and disciplines with advanced academic search tools.
Impact Analytics
Track how your publications perform — views, citations, and downloads — with real-time researcher dashboards.
Review Workflow
Built-in peer review tools — assign reviewers, track revisions, and manage editorial decisions in one place.
Institutional Archive
A permanent, searchable digital repository preserving AUCA's academic output for generations of students and scholars.
Collaboration Tools
Co-author papers, invite collaborators, and build cross-departmental research teams within the platform.
Simple process

From research to publication in three steps

1
Create your account
Sign up with your AUCA campus email and set up your researcher profile in minutes.
2
Submit your research
Upload your paper or thesis, add metadata, and submit it through our structured review pipeline.
3
Get published & cited
Your work goes live in the AUCA institutional archive, indexed and discoverable by the academic community.
